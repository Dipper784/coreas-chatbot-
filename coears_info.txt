DELIVERY POLICY:
- Orders are subject to product availability; if an item is out of stock, the customer is
  notified and refunded via original payment method.
- In-store pickup: pickup location is confirmed by email with the order confirmation.
- Pickup is typically fulfilled within 2 business days of ordering.
- Delivery typically takes 2-3 business days from order date.
- Products may be delivered separately.
- Pickup requires a valid ID and the order confirmation email.
- To check delivery status: email coreasbuilding@thegelgroup.com

RETURN POLICY:
- No returns/refunds on goods damaged after delivery to the customer.
- Returns require the Original Invoice and must be made within 7 days of purchase.
- Non-returnable: gift cards, some health/personal care items, items not in original
  condition or missing parts (not due to Coreas's error).
- No refunds on sale items.
- Approved refunds are credited to the original card or issued by cheque — no cash refunds.
- Refunds typically post within 7-14 working days; contact the bank first if delayed, then
  Coreas at coreasbuilding@thegelgroup.com or (784) 434-1224.
- Exchanges only for defective/damaged items — email, call, or visit the pickup location.

location to order.
